You are a food truck entrepreneur competing to expand your food truck fleet and create the most valuable food truck empire! Explore the city, selling your food to hungry customers to grow your brand - but watch out, the other food truck operators in the city might be up to mischief!


Flat tires, speeding tickets, staff strikes, and sinkholes are just some of the impediments that might get in your way. Keep your momentum going with a strategic application of food truck festivals, building billboards, award winning recipes, and more!

You will need to provide:
- Two Dice (of different colors)
- - The small Customer Type Icons and the Food Type Icons on the Trucks and Components print sheet can be glued or taped to the two dice.
- - OR - Assign each type icon a number value (1-6) and refer to the icons sheet as you roll the dice during the game.


Overview


Roll the Food Type and Customer Type dice each turn to find out which type of food is in-demand and which customer types are especially hungry.

Collect money for selling your food, and use that money to buy more food trucks to expand your offerings and bring your delicious food to more customers throughout the city.

As you drive your trucks around - you will reveal city block tiles and find new customers.

Additionally, you will have the chance to both influence the customers of the city and interfere with your opponents' schemes using Action Cards!

Once the city has been fully explored - the player with the most food trucks (and the most money) is the victor!

Refer to the Rulebook pdf document above for the full rules.
