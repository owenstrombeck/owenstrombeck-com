One morning, while brainstorming with Sheri about themes for a new game, she said - "There should be more games about pizza!", and over the next 30 minutes we outlined the mechanics that would turn into Sabotage Pizza.


You are the chef at a new pizza restaurant, trying to build a reputation of delivering the best pizzas in town - but watch out - your competition is willing to play dirty. Each turn you will add one topping to your pizza, and one topping on an opponent’s pizza!



Try to build a combination of toppings on your pizza that fulfills one of the available orders. When you deliver your pizza, that customer will give you more stars in their review if you delivered what they wanted. The chef with the most stars at the end wins!

Basic Rules

Refer to the Rulebook pdf document above for the full rules.


Turn Details

1. Play a Topping on Your Pizza
- Play one Topping Card from your hand face-up in front of you on the table.

2. Play a Topping on Someone Else’s Pizza
- Play one Topping Card from your hand on to someone else’s pizza (face-up in front of them).

3. Deliver Your Pizza?
- If your pizza now has 4 toppings on it, deliver it to one of the available orders!
- Collect stars for how well your pizza matches the order
- +1 or +2 stars for each topping on your pizza that matches the order.
- -1 star for each negative topping on your pizza.

4. Draw Topping Cards
- Draw Topping Cards from the Toppings Deck to fill your hand back up to 4 cards.


Winning the Game

- The first player to collect 20 stars wins!
- If the Orders Deck runs out before a player has collected 20 stars, whoever has the most stars when the last Order card is drawn from the Orders Deck is the winner!
