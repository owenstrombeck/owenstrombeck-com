About

Who is Mercury Crossing?

Formed in the late 2000's by Sheri Roloff, Mercury Crossing started out as a singer-songwriter styled outlet for the stories Sheri had to tell on the now hard to find first studio album - Human Aquarium. With the addition of Owen Strombeck on drums before recording their sophomore album - Social Graces Set Aside, the musical styling took a turn and headed in a classic rock and roll direction that has carried throughout their third and fourth studio releases - Red July and The Takeover.
